Product Variant's Price Extra

Set and Manage different Extra Price for a Product Variant

The Odoo Product Variant Extra Price Module will assist you in quoting an additional price for a particular product variant. Aside from the extra price of an attribute, the seller can set a price that is lower or higher for a specific product variant.

Set a higher price for higher profits in Odoo!!

Let's imagine that the seller has added a premium to the price of a women's dress because it is black, and he also wants to add a premium to the price of a certain product variant because it is the most popular, such as a medium-sized black dress. Installing this module will make it easy to accomplish this.

It assists you in managing or setting an additional price for a certain product version. No matter the attribute or attribute value, you can set the extra price to be charged on a product's version.

The additional cost is valid throughout Odoo. At the point of sale, an additional price is immediately imposed. There is no requirement for manual involvement. The additional cost for the product's version is relevant to website sales as well.
